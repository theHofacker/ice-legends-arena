# Mutant Hockey Federation

**Play to Save Earth**

A dystopian post-apocalyptic 5v5 hockey mobile game where mutants battle alien champions to save Earth from occupation.

## Overview

In an alternate timeline, Earth faces alien domination through sports combat. After the Thousand Year War created a race of mutant humans with incredible abilities, these enhanced warriors became humanity's last hope. Now, 100 years after the first legendary match, the galaxy returns for a rematch.

Choose from 8 unique mutant archetypes (Pyro, Flux, Rage, Phantom, Bulwark, Hive, Ricochet, Morph), build your team, and compete in fast-paced hockey matches with formation strategy, timing-based mechanics, and character abilities.

**See [LORE.md](./Ice%20Legends%20Arena/Docs/Lore.md) for complete game design and story details.**

## Game Features

- **8 Mutant Archetypes**: Unique playstyles and abilities
- **5v5 Formation Strategy**: Tactical team composition
- **Perfect Timing System**: Skill-based shooting and passing mechanics
- **Ethical F2P**: Player-first monetization with no pay-to-win
- **Franchise Vision**: Foundation for Mutant Sports Universe

## Vision

Mutant Hockey Federation is the first game in a planned franchise of mutant sports titles. Same characters, different sports - building a unified sports gaming universe.
