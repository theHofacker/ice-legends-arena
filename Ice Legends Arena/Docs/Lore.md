## **üåç Core Story Concept**

### **The Premise**

In an alternate universe, a group of aliens visit Earth. They are all from the same species but have slightly different abilities. The aliens ask to speak with Earth's leaders to discuss Earth's surrender. During their meeting, a hockey game plays in the background. Confusing the game for war, one of the alien generals challenges the human leader. Realizing this would probably be a game to the death (his death), he suggests they gather Earth's best athletes to compete in "battle."

However, in this universe, Earth has a race of humans with incredible abilities. While most of these people have horrible deformities, others look as normal as you or me (except for their eyes‚Ä¶ you can always tell a mutant from their eyes). These people, known as Mutants, were the product of the Thousand Year War‚Äîwhich ended with both nations' capitals being doused with toxic chemicals that killed millions instantly and transformed a small percent of the population into something else. The surrounding cities and wildlife were also changed, as the toxic waste ran through the water and land.

These special people‚Äî"Mutants"‚Äîgave Earth the strength to win. But this was just the beginning, as this was only one race of alien that only had a month to train. Quickly, word spread throughout the galaxy of a new game. First spreading along the galactic silk road, often passed along by merchants and bounty hunters to pass time. As merchants passed through war-torn solar systems, soldiers picked up the game and would play on passing comets. Soon the sport spread to larger cities when soldiers returned home. Then real athletes started to train and compete.

Over the next 100 years, the game grew in popularity and the legend of the Mutants grew larger than life‚Äîgiving rise to the **Mutant Hockey Federation (MHF)**. And to celebrate the 100th year anniversary, they decided to visit Earth for a rematch.

### **The Stakes**

In this universe, war drove humanity to create more advanced technology than we have today. So it's not uncommon for people to live to 150\. While the mutants who originally played that legendary game are still alive, they are in no playing shape. Instead, they became coaches, trainers, equipment CEOs, and team owners‚Äîgiving Earth the firepower and skill they'll need to compete in this new league and save Earth from doom.

**If Earth loses: Alien occupation.**  
 **If Earth wins: Freedom for another 100 years.**

**Common Tier (Launch Characters):**

* Enhanced eye effects (glow intensity, particle effects)  
* Minor deformities (extra finger, small tail, pointed ears)

**Epic Tier (Mid-Season Skins):**

* Visible mutations (glowing veins throughout body, crystalline growths)

* Extreme transformations (monstrous, fully mutated)

**Art Direction Philosophy:** "Start simple, scale complexity. Every mutant should be identifiable by silhouette and eye color alone."

### **Equipment System: Card-Based Progression**

**Post-Match Salvage (Loot System):**

After every match, mutants scavenge the arena for upgraded equipment left behind by competitors. This "salvage" comes in the form of Equipment Cards representing gear improvements.

**Equipment Slots (4 per character):**

1. **Stick** \- Offensive power (shot speed, damage)  
2. **Skates** \- Movement speed (acceleration, top speed)  
3. **Gloves** \- Puck handling (pass accuracy, deke success)  
4. **Helmet** \- Defensive resilience (check resistance, stamina)

**Rarity Tiers:**

* **Common** (Standard Issue) \- White glow \- Base stats  
* **Rare** (Modified Tech) \- Blue glow \- \+15% boost  
* **Epic** (Alien Hybrid) \- Purple glow \- \+30% boost \+ minor effect  
* **Legendary** (Pre-War Prototype) \- Orange glow \- \+50% boost \+ unique effect

**Acquisition Methods:**

**1\. Post-Match Chests (Primary Method \- FREE):**

* Every match awards a chest based on performance  
* **Victory Chest:** 5 equipment cards, 1+ Rare guaranteed  
* **Elite Chest:** 5 equipment cards, 1 Epic guaranteed (3+ win streak)  
* **Champion Chest:** 5 equipment cards, 1 Legendary guaranteed (ranked milestones)

**Chest Opening Experience:**

* Visual reveal animation (exciting moment\!)  
* Contents shown BEFORE opening (full transparency)  
* Duplicates auto-convert to Crafting Shards instantly  
* No inventory clutter, no manual management needed

**2\. Crafting System:**

* Duplicate cards ‚Üí Crafting Shards (salvage currency)  
* **Crafting Costs:**  
  * 10 Shards \= 1 Common card of choice  
  * 50 Shards \= 1 Rare card of choice  
  * 200 Shards \= 1 Epic card of choice  
  * 1,000 Shards \= 1 Legendary card of choice  
* Players choose exactly what equipment they want  
* No RNG, no waste

**3\. Direct Shop Purchase (Optional Shortcut):**

* Buy specific equipment cards without RNG  
* Uses Mutation Credits (premium currency, also earnable)  
* Example: 100 Credits \= 1 Epic equipment of choice  
* Skips the grind, doesn't provide advantage (same stats as earned)

**Ethical Design Principles:**

* ‚úÖ Primary progression path is FREE (play to earn)  
* ‚úÖ No gambling with real money (can't buy random chests)  
* ‚úÖ Duplicate protection built-in (auto-convert system)  
* ‚úÖ Can craft exact cards wanted (no RNG frustration)  
* ‚úÖ Optional direct purchase (convenience, not power)  
* ‚úÖ All equipment earnable through gameplay
